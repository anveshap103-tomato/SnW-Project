Cyber-Security

Stay safe and secure in the digital world.

Use Strong Passwords

Always use a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols. Avoid using the same password everywhere.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of protection to your accounts to keep hackers away.

Watch Out for Phishing

Never click on suspicious links or emails that ask for personal info.

Update Regularly

Keep your software, browser, and antivirus updated to patch vulnerabilities.

Stay Private Online

Don’t overshare on social media and use privacy settings wisely.

Use anti-virus and anti-malware

Install trusted security software and keep it updated to detect and remove threats automatically.

Scan external storage devices for viruses

Always scan USB drives and external hard disks before opening files to prevent infections.

Use a secure file-sharing solution to encrypt data

Share files through encrypted platforms to keep sensitive information safe from hackers.
